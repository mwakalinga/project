About Us

Bagasingile Company (BC)’s Electrical is involved with a variety of electrical work, ranging from: Domestic to Industrial Installations and Electrical Contracting. In our general electrical work, BC Electrical has successfully completed larger contracts with electrical consultants. Priding ourselves on quality workmanship and service excellence, Bc electrical is an authorized company and registered with all relevant Authorities. Situated in the lake zone/ Mwanza region, we have undertaken major projects throughout Tanzania.
